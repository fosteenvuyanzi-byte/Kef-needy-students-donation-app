How do you select students? +
We work with local schools and community leaders to identify bright students from low-income families who have demonstrated academic excellence but lack financial resources to continue their education.
Where does my money go? +
100% of donations go directly to student support including school fees, uniforms, learning materials, and sometimes meals. Our operational costs are covered by separate grants and board contributions.
Can I track my impact? +
Yes! We provide regular updates on the students you support, including academic progress reports and personal messages. You'll see exactly how your donation is making a difference.
Is KEF a registered charity? +
Yes, KEF is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. All donations are tax-deductible and you'll receive a receipt for tax purposes.
