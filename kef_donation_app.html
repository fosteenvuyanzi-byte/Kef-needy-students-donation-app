Meet James Mwangi
James dreams of becoming a doctor to serve his rural community in Kiambu County. As the eldest of five children, he excelled in primary school despite his family's financial struggles. His father, a small-scale farmer, earns just enough to feed the family, making secondary school fees impossible to afford.

When James received his KCPE results - an impressive 380 marks - his dreams seemed shattered. That's when KEF stepped in with a full scholarship, covering his school fees, uniforms, and learning materials. Today, James is thriving in Form 3, consistently ranking in the top 5 of his class.

"Without KEF, I would be working in the fields instead of preparing for university," James says with gratitude. "This scholarship isn't just changing my life - it's changing my entire family's future."
